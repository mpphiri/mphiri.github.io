I am a Ph.D. candidate in Applied Mathematics at the University of Alabama at Birmingham.

My research lies in applied analysis and nonlinear partial differential equations, with emphasis on fluid dynamics, turbulence, and regularity problems for Navier–Stokes-type systems.

I am also interested in mathematics education, teaching, and outreach, including creating accessible mathematics content for high school and college students.
