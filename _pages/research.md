My research is in applied analysis and nonlinear partial differential equations, with emphasis on mathematical fluid dynamics and regularity problems for Navier--Stokes-type systems.

## Research Interests

- Nonlinear partial differential equations
- Mathematical fluid dynamics
- Navier--Stokes regularity theory
- Hyperdissipative fluid models
- Analyticity and sparseness mechanisms
- Harmonic measure methods in PDE regularity
- Turbulence scales and higher-order derivative estimates

## Current Research

My current work investigates the interaction between analyticity radius estimates and sparseness structures in hyperdissipative Navier--Stokes systems. A central theme is how localized geometric depletion mechanisms may contribute to excluding finite-time blow-up in near-critical regimes.
